Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Is my medical report safe and private?
Your report is processed securely and permanently deleted after your explanation is sent. We never store, share, or sell your medical data. Your privacy is our top priority.
How long does it take to get my explanation?
Most explanations are delivered within 3–5 minutes of sending your report. You'll receive a confirmation message as soon as your report is received.
What if my report is in Hindi or Arabic?
We can process reports in English, Hindi, and Arabic. Explanations are sent back in clear English. Hindi explanations are available on the Family Pack plan.
How do I pay if I'm outside India?
International customers (UAE, Middle East, UK, Europe, etc.) pay via PayPal. No PayPal account needed. Click the link → select "Pay with Debit or Credit Card" → enter your card → done in 60 seconds. After payment you'll immediately receive our WhatsApp number.
Is this a replacement for my doctor?
No. MedSimple AI helps you understand your report so you can have a better conversation with your doctor. It is for educational purposes only and never replaces professional medical advice or diagnosis.
Can I use Family Pack for multiple WhatsApp numbers?
Yes. The Family Pack covers up to 5 family members, each on their own WhatsApp number. You can send reports for your parents, spouse, or children — all covered under one plan.
